Voice Command Help
"Add [task name] to [category name]"
Adds a task to the specified category with today's date.
Example: "Add finish report to Work"
"Add [task name] to [category name] on [date] at [time]"
Adds a task to the specified category with the given date and time.
Date format: MM/DD/YYYY (e.g., 12/25/2023)
Time format: HH:MM AM/PM (e.g., 3:30 PM)
"Add [task name] on [date] at [time]"
Adds a task without a category with the given date and time.
Example: "Add call mom on 12/25 at 2 PM"
"What are my tasks due today?"
Lists all tasks due today.
"What are my upcoming tasks?"
Lists all upcoming tasks with their due dates.
"Help"
